Internship & Research Experience

Embedded Software Development Intern, Dalian Institute of Chemical Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences
Battery Thermal Runaway Project, Academician Zhongwei Chen's Team   |   May 2026 - Aug. 2026

Worked on an implantable multi-source data acquisition platform for early warning of thermal runaway in new-energy vehicle batteries. I independently designed a high-integration embedded PCB, completed schematic and layout design, and verified signal integrity using oscilloscopes and multimeters. On the software side, I developed a multi-task real-time acquisition system based on STM32F4 and FreeRTOS, including drivers for SPI, IIC, CAN, USART, SD card storage, and multiple sensors. I also designed a tab-signal multiplexing scheme to encode and transmit sensing data through battery tabs under space-constrained conditions.
